Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection & Storage

NurtureCalc does not require sign-up or accounts. We do not collect, store, or transmit any health data you enter into our calculators. All calculations happen instantly in your browser and are never sent to our servers. Your preferred unit metric and cookie consent status are saved securely in your browser's local storage.

2. Cookies & Analytics

We respect your privacy. Our site only loads essential functional cookies by default. Only if you explicitly click "Accept" on our cookie banner do we load third-party scripts. The cookies we may use include:

  • Consent Cookie: Stored in your local storage to remember your cookie preference.
  • Google AdSense Cookies: Used to serve relevant ads. Google uses caching and cookies based on your prior visits to our website.
  • Google Analytics: (If enabled) Used to anonymously measure overall website traffic.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Your Rights Under GDPR

For our users in the European Union and the UK, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data we hold about you. Because we do not store your health data, the only personal data we potentially process is generalized usage data (via Google Analytics) and IP addresses. You have the right to object to this processing.

4. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Contact Data Controller

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Controller at: hello@nurturecalc.com.
