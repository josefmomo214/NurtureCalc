Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collected

NurtureCalc does not require sign-up or accounts. Any data entered into our calculators (such as weight, age, or symptoms) is processed locally on your device specifically to provide you with your result. We do not store, track, or save this health data on our servers. Your preferred unit metric is saved securely in your browser's local storage.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site, which allows us to keep NurtureCalc free. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your internet browsing history.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@nurturecalc.com.
