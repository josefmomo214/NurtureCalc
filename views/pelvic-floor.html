Pelvic Floor Recovery Estimator

Your pelvic floor acts like a hammock holding your pelvic organs. Pregnancy and childbirth—regardless of delivery type—stretch and weaken these muscles. Recovery is absolutely possible, but recognizing your current stage is the vital first step.

Take this gentle 4-question quiz to estimate your recovery stage and understand when it might be time to seek a specialist's support.

1. How many weeks postpartum are you?

2. Do you leak urine when sneezing, coughing, or laughing?

3. Can you clearly feel your pelvic floor lift when you squeeze it (do a Kegel)?

4. Do you experience a feeling of pressure or 'heaviness' in your vagina?
