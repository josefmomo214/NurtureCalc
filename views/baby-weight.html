Baby Weight Percentile Calculator

Tracking your baby's growth provides a snapshot of their overall health and development. This calculator compares your baby's weight to standard World Health Organization (WHO) growth charts to determine their percentile.

A percentile simply shows where your baby falls compared to 100 other babies of the same age and sex. Remember, healthy babies come in all shapes and sizes—what matters most is that their individual growth curve is trending upwards consistently.
